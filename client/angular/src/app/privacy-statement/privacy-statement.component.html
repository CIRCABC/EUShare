CIRCABC Share Privacy statement

General

CIRCABC Share, as an application developed by the Commission of the European Union, is committed to user privacy.

Information Collection and Use

What information is stored?

While using the Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personal information may include, but is not limited, to your full name and email address.

Why do we store and process your data?

We retrieve and store your personal data for enabling you, as a user, to connect to the CIRCABC Share application and use the Service for creating and publishing online questionnaires. Your contact details (email address), first name and last name can also be used to contact you back for support and quality management purposes.

How long are the data stored?

EC staff member (internal user): As long as the user is an EC staff member, his personal settings and downloaded content remain available. A user can at any time ask the CIRCABC Share team to terminate his account if he no longer wishes to use the Service. The corresponding account, all associated data, and all shares and results will be permanently deleted.

Whom are your data disclosed to?

The staff managing the CIRCABC Share application have access to your personal data.

How to access or modify your data?

Your first name, last name and email address are taken from EULogin and can only be modified there.

Log Data

We collect information that your browser sends when you use CIRCABC Share ("Log Data").
